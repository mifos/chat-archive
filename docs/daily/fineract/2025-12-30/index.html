you can use the existing business step for this:
  • CHECK_LOAN_REPAYMENT_DUE -&gt; business event before X days of due date
  • CHECK_LOAN_REPAYMENT_OVERDUE -&gt; business event after X days of due date
  • CHECK_DUE_INSTALLMENTS -&gt; Business event about overdue installments
